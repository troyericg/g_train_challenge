
G TRAIN CHALLENGE APP: 

Purpose: To record and display the commute times for both Renda and Andrei, to see who's commute is quicker. 

Tools: Firebase, Grunt, Tabletop.js (backup)
Languages: HTML/CSS, JS


---------------------------------------

DETAILS: 

- Side-by-side comparison of each commute.

- The faster time would get highlighted. 

- Logging in would open up a drop down menu for entering your trip details

- Display emoji based on word input
